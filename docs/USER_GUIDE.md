## 1. Getting Started

### Creating an Account
1. Navigate to the landing page and click **Start Learning Free** or access the `/register` page.
2. Fill in your **Full Name**, **Email Address** (preferably your university `.edu` email), and a secure **Password**.
3. A password strength indicator will guide you to create a secure password.
4. Click **Create Account**. You will be automatically logged in and redirected to your dashboard.

### Logging In
1. If you already have an account, click **Student Login** from the landing page.
2. Enter your credentials and click **Log In**.

## 2. The Student Dashboard

Your dashboard is your central hub for tracking your progress and accessing learning materials.

* **Welcome Hero:** Displays your current level, total XP, and a visual ring showing your progress towards the next level.
* **Quick Stats:** At a glance, view your completed modules, your current daily login streak, and your average score across all quizzes.
* **Learning Modules Panel:** Shows all available modules. Modules must be completed sequentially; upcoming modules remain locked until you pass the preceding ones.
* **Badges Earned:** Displays the visual badges you unlock as you complete specific milestones.
* **Leaderboard:** See how your XP compares to the top 5 students on the platform.

---

## 3. Completing Learning Modules

The curriculum consists of interactive modules designed to test your ethical reasoning. Each module follows a 3-step process:

1. **Learn:** Read through the foundational concepts and guiding principles regarding the topic (e.g., Privacy, Bias, Transparency).
2. **Interactive Scenarios:** You will be presented with real-world scenarios. Read the prompt and select the most ethical response.
   * *Feedback:* Upon submitting a decision, the system will immediately provide feedback on why your choice was ethical, partially ethical, or highly unethical.
3. **Results:** After completing all scenarios, you will receive an overall percentage score. If you score high enough, you will earn XP and unlock the next module.

---

## 4. Gamification

The platform uses gamification to make learning engaging:

* **Experience Points (XP):** Earn XP by successfully completing modules. Higher scores in the scenario quizzes yield more XP.
* **Levels:** Accumulate XP to rank up. You start as a *Novice* and can eventually reach the rank of *Guardian*. Your rank is visible on the Leaderboard and in your navigation bar.
* **Daily Streaks:** Logging in on consecutive days builds your "Streak." Your active streak is proudly displayed with a fire icon in your navigation menu.
* **Badges:** Specific achievements grant you permanent badges (e.g., the *Social Good Advocate* badge).

## 5. The Administrator Portal

*Note: This section is only for users with Admin privileges.*

If your account has the `admin` role, an **Admin Portal** link will appear in your navigation bar and at the bottom of your dashboard.

### Admin Features
* **Global Analytics:** View high-level statistics including Total Students, Modules Completed, Average Scores, and overall platform Completion Rates.
* **Module Performance Charts:** Visual bar charts showing drop-off rates (how many students complete Module 1 vs Module 3) and average scores per module.
* **Student Tracking Table:** A searchable, sortable list of every registered student.
   * **Drill-down:** Click on any student row to expand their profile. You can view exactly which modules they have attempted, their specific score on that attempt, and the date they completed it.
